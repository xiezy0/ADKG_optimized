ADKG

We design and implement an improved ADKG protocol focusing on its most important metric— latency. We explore a novel and much more direct reduction from ADKG to the underlying blocks, significantly decreasing the computation overhead and communication rounds. Our protocol works for and improves on both the low threshold and high-threshold scenarios. Our protocol relies on the well-established discrete logarithm (DL) problem in the standard model, while prior works rely on stronger assumptions and random oracles.
